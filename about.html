About the company

Bondy Brewers© is the heart of every major North American coffee institution and the home of safe, easy-to-use, and reliable coffee brewers. Bondy Brewers is committed to quality perfection by ensuring that we stive for excellence in all fields, understand the consumer and client needs to exceed expectations and continue to improve our quality driven practices.

Our Founder

Founded in 1918 by Johnathan H. Bondy at the end of the first world war. After returning from the war Johnathan was frustrated by how unreliable coffee brewers were at the time and how almost every pot had grinds in it unless you followed an EXACT difficult process. Using the skills, he learned developing and creating the "Jeffery Armored Car" in the war, Johnathan was able to develop the first in a long line of reliable and easy to use coffee brewers that has made a huge impact in the North American coffee industry.

Reasons to Choose Us

  • 100% North American Made Coffee Brewers
  • #1 brand among the competition
  • Strong ties to the community via our outreach programs
  • Tons of products for every consumer
  • Competitive Prices
